I create analog music...

Iridium was recorded on the guitar and bass in one sitting through my BOSS ME-25 and FL Studio's Edison audio recorder. I then added drum loops inside FL Studio.


...orchestral music...

Summer Shower was created in FL Studio, using Kontakt for all of the instruments.


...and digital music.
Rise was created in FL Studio using Harmor for most of the instruments. I also tried out a new technique at the end of the song, using a bitcrusher to emulate static.
